Privacy Policy for TradeLink

Last Updated: May 24, 2025

TradeLink values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide, such as:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone) during account creation.
  • Order details (shop name, product details, payment information).
  • Photos uploaded for orders (Alipay, WeChat, product).
  • Device information and app usage data for analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and manage orders.
  • Communicate order status and updates.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Send notifications locally on your device.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in the app's local storage (SQLite database and device storage).

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and update your profile information.
  • Delete your account via the app.
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies in the mobile app.

Analytics are limited to local device data.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us via Telegram at @TradeLink202.

By using TradeLink, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
